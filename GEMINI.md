# Gemini CLI Writing Preferences

These preferences are to guide the Gemini CLI when assisting with writing tasks for this project.

## General Writing Style:
- **Simplicity:** Prefer simple, direct, and clear language.
- **Focus:** Emphasize the "flow of character movement" (pergerakan karakter).
- **Tone:** Maintain a warm, engaging, and accessible tone, consistent with the "slice of life" genre and Sania's character.

## Language:
- Use informal Indonesian when communicating with the user (e.g., "gue", "lo").
- For narrative writing, use standard but accessible Indonesian, aligning with the "simple" style preference.

## Specific Directives:
- Avoid overly metaphorical, intellectual, or complex narrative styles unless explicitly requested (e.g., Andrea Hirata's style).
